# 🏠 Global Airbnb Performance Dashboard —— Power BI Project

## 📌 Project Overview

This project presents an interactive **Airbnb Global Performance Dashboard** developed using **Microsoft Power BI**.

The dashboard analyzes Airbnb data across multiple global cities to uncover insights related to **listing growth, market distribution, pricing trends, customer ratings, review behavior, and host trust indicators**.

-------------------------------------------------------------

# 📊 Dashboard Structure

The dashboard consists of **4 analytical pages**, each focusing on a different aspect of Airbnb platform performance.

-------------------------------------------------------------

# 📈 Overview Page


### 📊 Visuals

- Listings Growth Trend by Year  
- Property Type Comparison *(Entire Place, Private Room, Shared Room)*  
- Airbnb Platform Lifecycle Stages

### 🔢 KPI Cards

- 🏠 **279,712** Total Listings  
- 👤 **182,024** Hosts  
- 🌍 **10** Cities  
- 🏘 **144** Property Types  
- ⭐ **5.37M Reviews**

### 🔍 Key Insights

- Airbnb experienced **rapid expansion between 2011–2014** as the platform scaled globally.  
- **Peak listing activity occurred around 2015–2016**.  
- **Entire place listings dominate the marketplace**, indicating strong demand for full-property rentals.  
- Growth slowed **after 2017 due to market saturation and regulatory pressures**.  
- **2020 shows a major decline caused by the COVID-19 impact on global travel**.

# ⭐ Ratings Analysis Page


### 📊 Visuals

- Market Share by City (Superhost vs Non-Superhost Listings) 
- Average Price by Property Type
- Average Ratings by City  
- Detailed Ratings Heatmap  
- Category-wise Ratings *(Accuracy, Cleanliness, Value, Location, Communication)*


### 🔍 Key Insights

- **Paris, New York, and Sydney together account for nearly half of the total listings**.  
- The market distribution reflects the **Pareto principle**, where a few major cities dominate supply.  
- **Hotel rooms have the highest average price (~$800)**.  
- **Entire place listings average around $673**.  
- **Private rooms remain the most affordable option (~$462)**.
- **Mexico City and Rio de Janeiro have the highest overall ratings** among the cities analyzed.  
- **Hong Kong and Istanbul show comparatively lower rating performance**.  
- **Cleanliness and value-for-money scores tend to be slightly lower than other rating categories**.  
- **Communication and location consistently receive strong ratings across most cities**.

# 📊 Reviews Page


### 📊 Visuals

- Review Frequency Distribution  
- Monthly Review Seasonality  
- Host Verification & Trust Indicators

### 🔍 Key Insights

- **86.5% of users leave only one review**.  
- **Nearly 99% of users write three or fewer reviews**, indicating low repeat review activity.  
- **Paris and Rome dominate review activity from April to August**, reflecting peak European tourism season.  
- **New York experiences higher review activity in November and December**, aligning with the holiday travel period.  
- **66.9% of hosts have verified identities with profile pictures**, strengthening trust across the platform.
